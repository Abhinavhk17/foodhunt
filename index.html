VV puram

The street has over 20 food stalls in less than 150 meters serving varieties of street food, sweets, South Indian, North Indian and Chinese dishes. Called Thindi Beedi in Kannada and Khaau Gali in Hindi, the food street is said to have a cult status among Bengalureans.The street typically becomes busy after six in the evening.

Indhiranagar

Indiranagar, one of the Bengaluru's newer areas is a noted residential and commercial hub of the city. Mainly dotted with pubs, hotel chains, and shopping centres, Indiranagar is a cosmopolitan locality with an influx of migrants from around the country.

koramangala

Koramangala in Bangalore is a hub of hangout zones and exotic food options. And for any one travelling to Bangalore, the neighbourhood would provide a lot of what the city has to offer in terms of vibrant evenings, places to hangout at, or just to sample some good old tastes of India.
